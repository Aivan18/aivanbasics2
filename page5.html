Unrequited love: A Gay Man's Secret Crush

By Aivan

In secret, my heart beats fast, For a love that cannot last. A straight man, my crush, so true. But to him, my love, I cannot pursue.

I watch him from afar, And wonder what might be. If only he could see The love that burns within my heart.

But fear holds me back From confessing my love For fear of losing him And the friendship we have built.

So I keep my feelings locked away And hope that someday, He'll see me in a different light And my love for him will take flight.

But until that day arrives I'll keep my love locked deep inside And hope that one day he'll see The love that burns so bright.
